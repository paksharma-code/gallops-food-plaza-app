# Privacy Policy — Gallops Food Plaza

**Effective date:** 28 April 2026  
**Last updated:** 28 April 2026

Gallops Food Plaza ("we", "our", "us") operates the Gallops Food Plaza mobile application (the "App"). This page informs you of our policies regarding the collection, use, and disclosure of personal data when you use the App, and the choices you have associated with that data.

We use your data to provide and improve the App. By using the App you agree to the collection and use of information in accordance with this policy.

---

## 1. Information we collect

When you use the App, we may collect the following information that you voluntarily provide:

### 1.1 Offer claims
When you claim a daily offer through the App we collect:
- **Name**
- **Mobile / WhatsApp number** (required, for delivering your offer token)
- **Date of birth** (optional, for special offers / birthday rewards)
- **Anniversary date** (optional, for special offers)

### 1.2 Reservations
When you book a table at a participating outlet we collect:
- **Name, mobile number, party size, date, time, and outlet selection**

### 1.3 Feedback
When you submit feedback we collect:
- **Name, mobile (optional), email (optional), star rating, and your written feedback**

### 1.4 Notify-me requests
For upcoming plazas you can ask to be notified — we then store **name, mobile, plaza of interest**.

### 1.5 Automatic device data
Like most apps, basic technical information may be logged automatically (device type, OS, app version, anonymous crash reports) for stability and improvement of the App. This data does not personally identify you.

### 1.6 What we do NOT collect
- We do not collect your precise GPS location.
- We do not access your contacts, calendar, microphone, or photos unless you explicitly upload an image.
- We do not collect payment / financial information through the App. Payments at our outlets happen at the counter directly.

---

## 2. How we use your information

We use the information we collect to:

- Deliver your offer token via WhatsApp to the right plaza or outlet.
- Confirm and manage your reservations.
- Respond to your feedback and improve our service.
- Notify you about upcoming plazas you've subscribed to.
- Provide and maintain the App.
- Detect, prevent, and address technical issues.

We do NOT sell your personal data to third parties for marketing.

---

## 3. Sharing your information

Your data may be shared with:

- **The specific Gallops plaza or outlet** you are interacting with (e.g. for an offer claim or reservation), so they can serve you.
- **WhatsApp / Meta** — when you click "Send via WhatsApp" the App opens WhatsApp directly. WhatsApp's own privacy policy then applies to that conversation.
- **Analytics providers (future)** — anonymous usage statistics may be sent to Google Firebase Analytics or similar tools to help us understand how the App is being used. This data is aggregated and does not identify you personally.
- **Legal authorities** — if required by Indian law, court order, or other valid legal process.

We do not share your contact details with any third-party advertiser or unrelated business.

---

## 4. Data retention

- **Offer claim tokens** are valid until end-of-day IST and automatically expire.
- **Reservations** are retained until the date of your booking and for a reasonable period after for service quality review.
- **Feedback** is retained as long as needed to act on it.
- **Notify-me requests** are retained until the corresponding plaza opens, after which they are deleted.

You can request deletion of your personal data at any time using the contact details below.

---

## 5. Children's privacy

The App is not directed to children under 13. We do not knowingly collect personal information from children under 13. If you are a parent or guardian and you believe your child has provided us with personal data, please contact us so we can delete it.

---

## 6. Security

We take reasonable measures to protect the personal information we collect, including secure transport (HTTPS / TLS) for all data in transit and access controls on our backend systems. However, no method of transmission over the internet is 100% secure, and we cannot guarantee absolute security.

---

## 7. Your rights

You have the right to:

- Access the personal data we hold about you.
- Request correction or deletion of your data.
- Withdraw any consent you have given (e.g. opt out of "notify-me" requests).
- Lodge a complaint with the appropriate data-protection authority.

To exercise any of these rights, contact us at the details below.

---

## 8. Changes to this policy

We may update this Privacy Policy from time to time. We will notify you of any changes by posting the new policy on this page and updating the "Last updated" date above. Continued use of the App after changes constitutes acceptance of the updated policy.
